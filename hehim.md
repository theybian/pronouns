# he/him

subject pronoun | he
object pronoun | him
possessive determiner | his
possessive determiner | his
reflexive pronoun | himself

## example
```markdown
**He** woke up at 7 am.
I will meet **him** later.
*He* told me **his** name was Sev.
That notebook I found yesterday was **his**.
*He* said he would finish it all by **himself**.
```
